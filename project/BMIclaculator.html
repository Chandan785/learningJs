BMI Calculator




BMI Weight Guide:
 Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 24.9
Overweight: 25 ≤ BMI < 29.9
Obesity: BMI ≥ 30

How to Use:
1. Enter your weight in kilograms.
2. Enter your height in meters.
3. Click "Calculate BMI" to see your result.
4. Click "Reset" to clear the form.

Note: This calculator is for informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
